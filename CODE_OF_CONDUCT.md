# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- Be welcoming
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Community leaders will follow guidelines when determining consequences.
